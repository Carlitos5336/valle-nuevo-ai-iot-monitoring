# 🌿 AI & IoT Monitoring System for Protected Areas  
### Case Study: Valle Nuevo National Park (Dominican Republic)

This repository contains the implementation and documentation of a final degree project focused on improving environmental monitoring and risk detection in protected areas through Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things (IoT).

---

## 📘 Project Overview

Protected areas in the Dominican Republic face constant threats such as:

- Wildfires  
- Illegal logging  
- Conuquismo (unauthorized agriculture)  

This project proposes a **smart monitoring system** that combines:

- 📡 IoT-based environmental stations  
- 🤖 Artificial Intelligence models  
- 📊 Data visualization through Power BI  

The goal is to **detect risks early, prevent environmental damage, and enable faster response** through both proactive and reactive mechanisms.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Optimize monitoring processes in protected areas  
- Detect environmental risks in real time  
- Support prevention of wildfires and illegal activities  
- Provide actionable insights through data visualization  

---

## 🧠 Solution Architecture

The proposed system includes:

1. **IoT Stations**  
   - Environmental sensors deployed in the park  
   - Continuous data collection  

2. **AI Models**  
   - Detection of anomalous patterns  
   - Risk prediction and classification  

3. **Monitoring & Alerts**  
   - Preventive alerts based on risk levels  
   - Reactive alerts when anomalies are detected  

4. **Dashboard (Power BI)**  
   - Visualization of environmental conditions  
   - Risk indicators and trends
